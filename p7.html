New York City, NY, United States

The One World Trade Center is the tallest building in the U.S. The address is 1 World Trade Center, NY, United States. It was design by Skidmore, Owings & Merrill LLP, the same designer that design the tallest building in the world (Burj Khalifa). The One World Trade Center was a 8 year project that started in 2006 and was completed in 2014.It stands at 1,792 ft.

The One World Trade Center is one of New York's skyline located in downtown Manhatttan. For some it serves as a new civic icon for the country. It is a an architechural landmark that resembles the future and hope as it rises towards the sky.
