Dubai, United Arab Emirates

Burj Khalifa is the tallest building in the world. The address is 1 Emaar Boulevard, Dubai, United Arab Emirates. It was design by Skidmore, Owings & Merrill LLP and it was a 6 year project that started in 2004 and was completed in 2010.

It stands as the tallest with 160 stories at the height of 2,723 ft to the tip.It has 900 apartments, 304 hotel rooms, and 2,957 parking spaces.

(360 Degrees of Learning) At the top of Burj Khalifa, level 124 view the spectaular panorma from the observatory room. Tourist visit it everyday to learn the journey of Dubai's Arabian heritage and the story of Burj Khalifa.
