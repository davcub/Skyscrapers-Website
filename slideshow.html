Structures

In the past humans were building pyramids, obelisks, statues, and other structures. The structures symbolized their intelligences, powers, religions, and their outstanding abilities to convey messages to everyone who witnessed it. Today structures rise and fall around the world illustrating our capabilities. Structures that you would think that are impossible to be build are being built.

There are structures around the world that are a work of art. They stand tall as if they can almost reach the sky. Every measurements and pieces are precisely put together for everyone to witness the colossal achievement of mankind. The unimaginable effort put into these structures convey mankind's potential.

These structures today stand tall in cities around the world. Looking at the past they have come a long way, yet I believe they have not scratch the surface of their potential. This webpage features these beautiful and stunning structures that continues to rise.

~David Cubrilla
