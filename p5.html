Shanghai, China

The Shanghai Tower is the tallest building in Asia. The address is Lujiazui Financial Center, Pudong New Area, Shanghai, China. It was design by Genseler, a company well known in Asia for building tall towers. It was a 6 year project that started in 2009 and was completed in 2015.

It stands as the second tallest building in the world at 2,073 ft. It has 132 floors, 258 hotel rooms, and 1,794 parking spaces.

It is the 3rd tower of the trio, Lujiazui Finance and Trade Zone. It is placed near Jin Mao Tower and Shanghai World Financial Center, yet it is rises above them with ease.
