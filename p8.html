Taipei, Taiwan

The TAIPEI 101 is the 5th tallest building in the world. The address is No.7 Xin Yi Road, District Xin Yi, Taipei, Taiwan. It was design by C.Y.Lee & Partners Architects/Planners and it was a 5 year project that started in 1999 and was completed in 2004. It represents the concept of striving for beyond perfection.

The structure has multiple uses; house retail facilities, fitness center, restaurants, observation decks, and communication facilities. The structure stands at 1,667 ft. with a transit station beneath the building. In the 87th floor, a 900 ton tuned mass damper is installed to counter earthquakes and typhoons.

Taipei has the fastest elevators in the world, rising at 1010 meters per minute. The structure is influenced by the spirit and culture of the Chinese, having bamboo on the outside spire. The pagoda shape is also well seen throughout the outside and inside of the building. Also the number 8 is incorporated throughout the building due to the fact that the number 8 is the lucky number for the Chinese.
